Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 17, 2026

Agentivo ("we", "our", or "us") operates the WhatsApp scheduling assistant. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

When you use our WhatsApp Flow, we collect:

  • Phone number (provided by WhatsApp)
  • Event details you submit (name, description, dates, time slots)
  • Participant names and availability selections

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Create and manage scheduling events
  • Calculate optimal meeting times based on participant availability
  • Send confirmation messages via WhatsApp

Data Storage and Retention

Event data is stored securely and retained only for the duration needed to facilitate scheduling. Events are automatically deleted 30 days after the last selected date.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • With WhatsApp/Meta as required to operate the service
  • When required by law

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us. Under GDPR, EU residents have additional rights including access, rectification, and portability.

Contact

For questions about this policy or to request data deletion, contact us through the WhatsApp service.

Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
